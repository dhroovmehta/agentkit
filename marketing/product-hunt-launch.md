## Strategy Notes

### What Will Work
- **Authenticity:** You built these. Share the real story (18 months, 10 agents, patterns learned). People buy from builders, not marketers.
- **Specificity:** Don't say "automate your business." Say "automate 8 departments with one agent." Concrete beats abstract.
- **Proof:** Show the ROI clearly (40+ hours saved, 15+ hours/week labor savings). Numbers stick.
- **Engagement:** Respond to EVERY comment in the first 24 hours. PH rewards engagement. Shows you're a real person, not a bot.

### What Won't Work
- **Over-selling:** Don't claim AI agents solve everything. Acknowledge limitations (human review needed, needs real data, etc.).
- **Vagueness:** "AI-powered templates" is meaningless. "Pre-built n8n workflows with optimized system prompts" is clear.
- **Spam:** Don't ask people to hunt or upvote multiple times. Vote once. Move on.
- **Ignoring feedback:** Someone will find a problem. Fix it fast, document the fix, move on.

### Likely Objections (Prepare Responses)
1. **"Why should I buy this vs. building my own?"**
   - Answer: "40 hours of engineering per template. You can buy the shortcut or learn by doing. Both valid. This is for people who want the shortcut."

2. **"Can I customize these?"**
   - Answer: "Yes. Designed for it. Each template comes with a customization guide. The system prompt is annotated."

3. **"Do I need to code?"**
   - Answer: "No. n8n is visual. System prompts are text. No code needed to deploy."

4. **"What if I don't use Claude?"**
   - Answer: "Built for Claude + MCP, but you can adapt the system prompts for any LLM. The architecture is model-agnostic."

5. **"What's the pricing? Is there a bundle?"**
   - Answer: "Free template (Solo Ops Agent) to try. Paid templates $29-$79. Bundle packages at $149-$199 for 3+ templates."
